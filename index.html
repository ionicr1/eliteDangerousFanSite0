Elite Dangerous is a space-simulator MMO. You take control of a "Commander"
which you will then use to partake onto missions with. The choices at your
disposal range from pirate work, bounty hunter, a miner, or a simple explorer.
Others simply have taken it upon themselves simply to help new players in the game.
